1. About

Hello, here is the my contact and info page. My name is Mikhail Efremov, now I’m kind of nomad. Have BS, MS and finished, but not defended PhD in Software Engineering and CS. My research work was related to machine learning approaches for swarm robotic foraging problem, then had evolved into federated learning, but now my most interested domain is distributed DBMS. I’m contributing in Apache Ignite 3 and love it.
